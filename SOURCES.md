## Japanese Cultural & Religious Sources

> - Respect kitsune as part of living Japanese religious tradition

- *Nihon Ryōiki* (日本霊異記) - "Miraculous Stories from the Japanese Buddhist Tradition" (early 9th century). Early kitsune accounts.
- Various Heian and Edo period literature featuring kitsune tales.
